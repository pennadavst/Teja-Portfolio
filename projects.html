Throughout my career, I have developed and contributed to a wide range of projects that demonstrate my technical expertise and problem-solving skills across cybersecurity, web development, and optimization. Below are some highlights of these projects:

Phishing Simulation and Malware Injection

Simulated a sophisticated phishing attack that involved gaining unauthorized access to a target's device, remotely executing code, injecting malware, and monitoring the entire process using the Splunk SIEM solution. This project provided valuable insights into phishing vectors and defensive strategies.

Social Engineering Attack Simulation

Designed and executed a social engineering attack that tricked a user into clicking a malicious link embedded in a legitimate-looking fake website. Once access was gained, I installed a keylogging software to record the victim’s keystrokes, simulating the full lifecycle of such attacks to improve threat detection capabilities.

Ransomware Forensics

Led a ransomware forensics initiative, utilizing a range of digital investigative tools to thoroughly analyze and trace the origins of ransomware attacks. This involved identifying the root cause of the breach, examining which processes were active on infected devices during the malware's presence, and assessing system behavior before, during, and after the attack.
