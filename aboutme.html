My name is Teja, and I am deeply passionate about cybersecurity. While my academic background, including both my bachelor's and master's degrees, is rooted in computer science and primarily focused on development, I chose to explore a new path when I decided to study in Canada. Initially, I enrolled in a computer application program at Conestoga College, which included a cybersecurity component. This sparked my interest in the field, leading me to pursue a full-fledged cybersecurity course at Durham College as my second program.


During my academic journey, I had the opportunity to participate in various projects, collaborating with individuals from diverse cultural and professional backgrounds. This experience not only broadened my perspective but also allowed me to take on leadership roles, guiding teams towards successful outcomes. I have honed strong communication skills and developed the ability to quickly adapt to new challenges and environments. My passion for continuous learning drives me to stay updated on industry trends and innovations. Whether working independently or as part of a team, I am confident in my ability to perform efficiently with minimal supervision. I consistently stay ahead of deadlines, strategically prioritizing tasks based on company goals and the potential impact of their completion on overall business success.


To unwind, I enjoy playing games and sports. My favorite game is billiards, while cricket is my favorite sport. In addition to these, I also like playing video games and have recently started learning badminton and tennis. Beyond playing, I love watching professional athletes in action, regardless of the sport.
